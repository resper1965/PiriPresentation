SUGGESTED STRATEGIC ACTIONS
MITIGATING THE 20.6% TECHNICAL READJUSTMENT
Clinical Management Financial Design
Network Optimization Employee Engagement
•Oncology: Implementation of specialized detection & support programs.
•High Users: Monthly monitoring and case-management for chronic/severe cases.
•Telemedicine: Strong promotion to reduce ER visits and elective costs.
•ER Cap Removal: Replace the R$150 limit with a standard 20% copay to control waste.
•Executive Copay: Implement cost-sharing in the CP8X plan to align with market.
•Plan Tiering: Introduce a "Mid-Tier" plan for Administrative/Ops elegibility.
•Referred Network: Incentive campaigns to use high-quality accredited hospitals instead 
of reimbursement.
•Reimbursement Control: Audit therapies reimbursements (87% of total) to detect 
potential fraud/waste.
•Benefits Communication: "Smart Use" webinars and infographics.
•Certificate Cross-Check: Integrate occupational health and certs to analyze 
absenteeism drivers.
